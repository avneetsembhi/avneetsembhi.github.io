🍪 Chocolate Chip Cookies

This is my favorite recipe — simple, soft, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 egg
  • Chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Mix all ingredients
  3. Shape cookies
  4. Bake for 10–12 minutes
